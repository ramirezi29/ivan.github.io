About

I'm Ivan Ramirez, a passionate iOS Engineer currently contracting with Apple, where I help shape the future of developer documentation for VisionOS, WidgetKit, and MapKit.

With over six years of experience and a background in psychology, I specialize in building intuitive, human-centered iOS applications. My work blends technical precision with strong UI/UX design principles inspired by Apple’s Human Interface Guidelines.

At Apple, I craft and refine documentation and sample projects used by millions of developers worldwide. Previously, I developed apps for First United Bank, significantly improving performance and stability for over 17,000 users.

I also run my own consultancy, Creative Apps, where I’ve brought client visions to life and published apps on both the App Store and Google Play with a 100% success rate.

I love mentoring, and as an iOS SME at Strategic Education, I helped more than 40 students launch their own apps. I’ve taught SwiftUI, UIKit, RESTful APIs, CI/CD, and more.

Outside of work, I enjoy exploring how design psychology influences app behavior and crafting digital tools that solve real human problems.
